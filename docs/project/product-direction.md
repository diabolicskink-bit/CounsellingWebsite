# Practice Facts

- Vive Counselling is Joel Griffiths' counselling practice.
- Joel works with adults.
- His approach is psychodynamic and attachment-informed.
- He works with concerns including anxiety, low mood, shame, self-criticism, trauma, relationship difficulties, grief, and burnout.
- Kink, BDSM, ENM, polyamory, sexuality, and LGBTQIA+ lives can be subjects or context in counselling.

# Exact-query and visible search landscape: Australian kink-aware counselling

Observed: 2026-07-17

## Scope and boundary

This is a dated sample of the visible results returned for six exact queries about kink-aware counselling or therapy for Australian adults. The material segment includes clinical counselling, psychotherapy, psychology, sex therapy, or sexology that explicitly names kink, BDSM, fetish, power exchange, or D/s and serves Australia. Perth/Western Australia and Australia-wide online delivery are both material.

The result classes used below are:

- **Direct clinical provider:** an owned provider page visibly describing counselling, psychotherapy, psychology, sex therapy, or sexology together with kink/BDSM/fetish/D/s language and an Australian service context.
- **Directory/intermediary:** a category, listing, or individual profile hosted by a directory, professional finder, or intermediary. A directory-hosted profile remains an intermediary result even when the listed person appears to offer a direct clinical service.
- **Adjacent coaching/education:** coaching, workshops, somatic education, or relationship education without a clearly visible clinical offer in the sampled result.
- **Professional/academic/informational:** professional guidance, research, institutional information, news, or general educational publishing rather than a clinical provider offer.
- **Irrelevant to the defined segment:** a returned result that does not visibly name kink/BDSM/fetish/D/s, is general relationship or mental-health support, or otherwise falls outside the boundary.

Classification uses only the returned title and snippet, plus another snippet for the same URL in this six-query sample where noted. It is not a judgement of credentials, competence, quality, availability, or actual client eligibility. Adult orientation is suggested by the relationship and sexual-therapy context, but minimum ages were not verified from the result interface.

## Search method and limits

- **Interface/tool:** OpenAI web-search tool, `search_query`.
- **Request configuration:** one exact query per request; `response_length: short`; no domain or recency filter.
- **Observation date:** 2026-07-17. The tool exposed crawler freshness labels for some results, but not a result-generation timestamp or a browser search-engine identity.
- **Comparable depth:** the first 12 numerically indexed general-web entries (`search0` through `search11`) for each query, 72 page-level entries in total. The tool returned additional mixed entries for most queries; these are reported as returned totals and sampled separately below, but are outside the fixed 12-entry comparison.
- **Ordinal:** the table number follows the tool's numeric result index, not a claimed Google or other search-engine rank. The rendered tool response sometimes displayed a supplemental item before a lower-numbered general-web entry.
- **Deduplication:** page-level visibility is the unit in each query, so different URLs from the same domain and different pages from the same provider are retained. For cross-query domain synthesis, `www`, trailing slashes, fragments, and query parameters are ignored; different URLs on one domain remain different page appearances. No exact URL appears twice within one fixed-depth query.
- **Requested geography:** expressed only in the query text. Five queries request Australia nationally; one requests Perth. The interface exposed no country/location parameter, so it cannot confirm an Australian search origin or a Perth-local search context.
- **Language and account state:** English is implicit in the query. The interface exposed no search-engine account, login, language, device, browser, IP location, SafeSearch, personalisation, or search-history state. All are unknown rather than controlled.
- **Dynamic features:** the tool returned link-and-snippet entries and did not expose ads, maps/local packs, autocomplete, related searches, People Also Ask, result-count estimates, or a conventional browser SERP. Their absence from this interface sample does not show absence on a live search engine.
- **Volatility and completeness:** results, order, snippets, crawler freshness, and dynamic features can change. This is not a complete provider census and does not establish rankings, query volume, demand, market share, service uptake, or audience preference.

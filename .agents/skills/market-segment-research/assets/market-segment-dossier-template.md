## Detailed market evidence

Replace this instruction with descriptive headings shaped by the research.
Use only the modules relevant to the brief; remove empty or unresearched
sections. Possible modules include:

- query and visible search landscape;
- demonstrable demand evidence;
- provider supply and competitor sample;
- directories, marketplaces, associations, and taxonomies;
- roles, delivery models, offers, pricing, and booking routes;
- adjacent services, substitutes, distribution, and referral channels; and
- official statistics, industry evidence, and market-size calculations.

Use common-field tables for repeated comparisons. State the unit, inclusion
rule, denominator, and observation date for every count. Keep search
visibility, demand, uptake, supply, market size, and preference distinct.
Attribute provider and directory claims rather than presenting them as verified
quality or competence.
